##  Ministry of Energy and Power Development

Site URL: https://www.energy.gov.zw
Reports To: [[President]]; [[Cabinet of Zimbabwe]]
Executive Position: [[Minister of Energy and Power Development]]

#### Function
Formulate and implement energy policy, oversee electricity and petroleum sectors, promote renewable energy, coordinate national energy planning, facilitate investment and supervise energy sector entities.
