## Description

Formulates and implements electricity policy, oversees power generation and distribution entities (ZESA, ZPC, ZETDC, ZERA, ZRA), monitors power supply, coordinates power sector reforms, develops energy infrastructure, promotes investment in electricity generation, and manages rural electrification programmes.
