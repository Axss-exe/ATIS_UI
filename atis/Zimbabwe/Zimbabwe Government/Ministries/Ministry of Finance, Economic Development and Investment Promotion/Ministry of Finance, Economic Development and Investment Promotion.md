## Ministry of Finance, Economic Development and Investment Promotion

Site URL: https://zimtreasury.co.zw
Reports To: [[President]]; [[Cabinet of Zimbabwe]]
Executive Position: [[Minister of Finance, Economic Development and Investment Promotion]]

#### Function
Manage public finances, formulate fiscal policy, coordinate national development plans, prepare national budgets, mobilize domestic and international finance, oversee revenue collection and support economic growth and investment promotion.
