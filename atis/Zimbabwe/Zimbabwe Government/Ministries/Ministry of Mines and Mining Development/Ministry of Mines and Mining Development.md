##   Ministry of Mines and Mining Development

Site URL: https://www.mines.gov.zw
Reports To: [[President]]; [[Cabinet of Zimbabwe]]
Executive Position: [[Minister of Mines and Mining Development]]

#### Function
Formulate mining policy, administer mining laws, regulate mineral rights, attract mining investment, promote beneficiation and value addition, supervise mining state enterprises and support mineral resource development.
