## Explosives Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe Republic Police]]
**Legislation**: [[Explosives Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 14 to 21 working days


## Description
The **Explosives Permit** issued by the **Zimbabwe Republic Police** under the **[[Explosives Act]]** is a regulatory authorization that allows individuals, companies, or organizations to possess, store, transport, manufacture, or use explosive materials for legitimate purposes such as mining, construction, or public safety; the permit outlines strict conditions regarding the security of storage facilities, the qualifications of personnel handling the explosives, and the documentation required for each transaction, and it mandates periodic inspections and compliance reporting to ensure that all activities conform to national safety standards and legal obligations, thereby safeguarding public welfare while facilitating controlled industrial and commercial use of hazardous substances.


## Standard Operating Procedure
  - "1. Application to local ZRP"
  - "2. ZRP forwards to CID Minerals/Explosives unit"
  - "3. Magazine inspection"
  - "4. Security vetting of blaster"
  - "5. Issuance of permit"
