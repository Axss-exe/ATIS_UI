## Export Authorization

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Fidelity Gold Refinery]]; [[Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe (RBZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[Gold Trade Regulations]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Gold Registration
**Processing Time**: 14 to 30 working days


## Description
The Export Authorization issued by **Fidelity Gold Refinery** and the **Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe (RBZ)** under the [[Gold Trade Regulations]] is a formal permit that confirms the refinery’s compliance with Zimbabwe’s stringent controls on gold extraction, processing, and shipment, guaranteeing that each consignment meets the country’s quality, provenance and anti‑money‑laundering standards; the document records the volume, purity and destination of the gold, authorizes its legal export, and serves as a traceable record for customs and financial oversight, thereby facilitating transparent trade while protecting the nation’s mineral resources and revenue streams.


## Standard Operating Procedure
  - "1. Lodge application via Authorised Dealer (Commercial Bank)"
  - "2. Bank submits to RBZ Exchange Control"
  - "3. Review by RBZ"
  - "4. Issuance of Exchange Control Authority"

## Required Documents
  - "Application via Bank"
  - "Invoices/Agreements"
  - "Tax Clearance"
  - "Acquittal records for previous exports"

## Common Bottlenecks
  - "Strict forex regulations"
  - "Request for further documentation"
  - "Foreign currency availability"
