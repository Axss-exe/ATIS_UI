## Waste Management Licence

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Environmental Management Agency (EMA)]]
**Legislation**: Environmental Management Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 60 working days


## Description
The **Waste Management Licence** is a regulatory instrument granted by the **Environmental Management Agency (EMA)** under the **Environmental Management Act**, authorising businesses and organisations to collect, transport, treat, store, recover, recycle or dispose of waste in accordance with nationally prescribed standards; it obliges licence holders to implement robust waste‑handling procedures, maintain accurate records, submit regular compliance reports, and ensure that all activities minimise environmental impact and protect public health, while providing the EMA with the authority to monitor, audit, and enforce conditions through inspections, penalties or licence modifications if required.
