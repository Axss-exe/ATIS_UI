## Forestry Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Forestry Commission]]
**Legislation**: [[Forestry Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 30 to 45 working days


## Description
The **Forestry Commission** issues a Forestry Permit under the **[[Forestry Act]]** to authorize the lawful use, management, and exploitation of forest lands, ensuring that activities such as timber harvesting, planting, and conservation are conducted in accordance with sustainable practices, environmental safeguards, and statutory requirements; the permit delineates the specific area, duration, and conditions of operation, mandates compliance with best‑practice guidelines, and obliges permit holders to submit regular monitoring reports, thereby balancing economic interests with the protection of biodiversity and the long‑term health of the forest ecosystem.
