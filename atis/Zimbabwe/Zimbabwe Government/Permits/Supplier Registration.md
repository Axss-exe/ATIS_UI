## Supplier Registration

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Procurement Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (PRAZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[Public Procurement and Disposal of Public Assets Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 1 to 3 working days


## Description
The **Procurement Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (PRAZ)**, operating under the **Public Procurement Act**, administers a mandatory Supplier Registration system that serves as the primary gateway for firms wishing to engage in government‑contracted work; this registration verifies that suppliers meet statutory eligibility criteria, possess the requisite technical and financial capacity, and adhere to ethical standards, thereby ensuring transparency, fairness, and accountability in the procurement process while providing a centralized, searchable database that public entities can consult to select qualified vendors and monitor compliance throughout the contract lifecycle.


## Standard Operating Procedure
  - "1. Register on PRAZ portal"
  - "2. Select categories"
  - "3. Upload statutory docs"
  - "4. Pay category fees"
  - "5. Instant/Auto certificate generation"

## Required Documents
  - "Certificate of Incorporation"
  - "Tax Clearance (ITF263)"
  - "NSSA Clearance"
  - "Vendor Bank details"

## Common Bottlenecks
  - "Expired tax clearances"
  - "System downtime on PRAZ portal"
