## Prospecting Licence

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Ministry of Mines and Mining Development]]
**Legislation**: [[Mines and Minerals Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 90 to 180 working days


## Description
The **Prospecting Licence** is a statutory authorization granted by the **Ministry of Mines and Mining Development** under the provisions of the **[[Mines and Minerals Act]]**, allowing individuals or companies to explore a designated area for the presence of mineral resources; the licence delineates the geographic boundaries, duration, and permissible activities, obliges the holder to submit regular exploration reports, adhere to environmental and safety standards, and pay the requisite fees, while also providing the government with a mechanism to monitor and regulate mineral prospecting activities to ensure sustainable resource development and compliance with national mining policy.
