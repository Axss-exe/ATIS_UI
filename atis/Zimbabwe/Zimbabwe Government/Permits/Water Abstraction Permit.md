## Water Abstraction Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe National Water Authority (ZINWA)]]
**Legislation**: Water Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 30 working days


## Description
The **Zimbabwe National Water Authority (ZINWA)** issues a Water Abstraction Permit under the provisions of the **Water Act**, granting legal authority to individuals, companies, or institutions to withdraw a specified volume of surface or groundwater for domestic, agricultural, industrial, or commercial use; the permit delineates the permitted extraction rates, monitoring requirements, and compliance obligations, and it is contingent upon the applicant demonstrating sustainable water management practices, adherence to environmental safeguards, and payment of applicable fees, thereby ensuring that water resources are allocated responsibly and protected against over‑exploitation.
