## Export Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[MMCZ Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 10 to 14 working days


## Description
The **Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)** issues an Export Permit under the **[[MMCZ Act]]** to authorize the lawful shipment of mineral commodities from Zimbabwe to foreign markets; this permit confirms that the applicant has met all statutory requirements, including proof of ownership, compliance with quality and grading standards, payment of applicable royalties and taxes, and adherence to environmental and safety regulations, thereby ensuring that the export is conducted transparently, responsibly, and in alignment with national policy objectives aimed at maximizing revenue and promoting sustainable resource development.
