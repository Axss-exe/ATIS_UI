## PPP Approval

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency (ZIDA)]]; [[Ministry of Finance, Economic Development and Investment Promotion]]
**Legislation**: [[ZIDA Act]] & PPP Framework
**Prerequisite Permits**: Investment Approval
**Processing Time**: 7 to 21 working days


## Description
The **Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency (ZIDA)**, in conjunction with the **Ministry of Finance, Economic Development and Investment Promotion**, issues a Public‑Private Partnership (PPP) Approval that serves as the definitive endorsement for projects aligning with the strategic objectives of the ZIDA Act and the national PPP Framework; this approval confirms that a proposed partnership meets the rigorous criteria for economic viability, risk allocation, and regulatory compliance, and it authorises the participating private entity to proceed with investment, construction, operation, and maintenance under the stipulated contractual terms, thereby unlocking financing, guaranteeing legal certainty, and fostering sustainable development outcomes that contribute to Zimbabwe’s broader growth agenda.
