## Diamond Marketing Approval

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[MMCZ Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 10 to 14 working days


## Description
The **Diamond Marketing Approval** is a formal authorization granted by the **Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)** under the provisions of the **[[MMCZ Act]]**, which certifies that a diamond has been sourced, processed, and is ready for export in compliance with national regulations; this approval ensures that each stone meets the stringent quality, traceability, and ethical standards set by the corporation, thereby facilitating lawful trade, protecting the country’s mineral resources, and maintaining Zimbabwe’s reputation in the global diamond market.
