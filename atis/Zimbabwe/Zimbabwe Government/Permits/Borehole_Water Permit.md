## Borehole/Water Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe National Water Authority (ZINWA)]]
**Legislation**: Water Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 30 working days


## Description
The **Zimbabwe National Water Authority (ZINWA)** issues a Borehole/Water Permit under the provisions of the **Water Act**, granting legal authorization to drill, construct, and operate a borehole for water extraction; the permit delineates the permissible depth, location, and extraction volume, mandates compliance with environmental safeguards, and requires the holder to submit regular monitoring reports, ensuring sustainable use of groundwater resources while protecting the rights of downstream users and maintaining the integrity of the national water resource management framework.
