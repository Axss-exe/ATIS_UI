## Aviation Approval

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Civil Aviation Authority of Zimbabwe (CAAZ)]]
**Legislation**: Civil Aviation Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 60 to 90 working days


## Description
The **Aviation Approval**, issued by the **[[Civil Aviation Authority of Zimbabwe (CAAZ)]]** under the **Civil Aviation Act**, serves as the official legal and technical clearance required to construct and operate private or commercial airstrips in Zimbabwe. It certifies that the physical design, runway alignment, and geographical coordinates of the facility comply with international safety standards, ensuring that it will not conflict with existing commercial flight paths or restricted military airspace. Functioning as a critical checkpoint before physical construction begins, this approval validates the structural layout for aviation safety and requires a prior **EIA Certificate** along with background security clearances from the Ministry of Defense to protect national airspace integrity.
