## Private Network Licence

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[Postal and Telecommunications Act]] 
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 60 to 120 working days


## Description
The **Private Network Licence** is a regulatory instrument granted by the **Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ)** under the **[[Postal and Telecommunications Act]] [Chapter 12:05]**, authorising entities to build, operate and maintain closed, non‑public telecommunications infrastructures such as corporate intranets, data‑centres and specialised radio networks; the licence imposes strict compliance requirements on spectrum usage, interconnection standards and service quality, while also mandating that licence‑holders ensure their networks do not interfere with public telecommunications services and that they adhere to the authority’s monitoring, reporting and security protocols.
