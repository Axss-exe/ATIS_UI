## Registration of Mine

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Ministry of Mines and Mining Development]]
**Legislation**: [[Mines and Minerals Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Lease
**Processing Time**: 90 to 180 working days


## Description
The **Registration of Mine** is an official document issued by the **Ministry of Mines and Mining Development** pursuant to the **[[Mines and Minerals Act]]**, confirming that a mining operation has met all statutory requirements and is legally authorized to explore, extract, and process mineral resources within a defined area; the registration details the mine’s location, ownership, scope of activities, and compliance obligations, and serves as the primary legal instrument that enables the holder to obtain necessary permits, secure financing, and engage with stakeholders while ensuring adherence to environmental, safety, and revenue‑sharing regulations stipulated by the Act.
