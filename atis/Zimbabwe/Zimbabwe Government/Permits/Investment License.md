## Investment License

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency (ZIDA)]]
**Legislation**: [[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: None
**Processing Time**: Varies by project complexity


## Description
The **Investment License** issued by the **Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency (ZIDA)** under the **[[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency Act]]** serves as the principal authorisation for both domestic and foreign investors to establish, expand, or restructure businesses in Zimbabwe, granting them preferential treatment such as tax incentives, duty exemptions, and streamlined regulatory procedures; it confirms that the proposed venture aligns with national development priorities, complies with sector‑specific regulations, and meets the agency’s criteria for economic impact, job creation, and technology transfer, thereby providing a legal framework that facilitates investment while ensuring accountability and transparency in the country’s growth agenda.
