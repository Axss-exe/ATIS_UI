## Rail Access Approval

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[National Railways of Zimbabwe (NRZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[Railways Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 14 to 30 working days


## Description
The **National Railways of Zimbabwe (NRZ)** issues a Rail Access Approval under the provisions of the **[[Railways Act]]** to grant third‑party operators the legal right to use its railway infrastructure for the movement of goods or passengers; the approval delineates the specific routes, time slots, safety standards, and fee structures applicable to the applicant, while also imposing obligations such as compliance with NRZ’s operational rules, insurance requirements, and liability provisions, thereby ensuring that all rail traffic is coordinated, secure, and consistent with national transportation policy.
