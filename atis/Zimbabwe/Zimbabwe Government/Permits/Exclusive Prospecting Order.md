## Exclusive Prospecting Order

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Ministry of Mines and Mining Development]]
**Legislation**: [[Mines and Minerals Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 90 to 180 working days


## Description
The **Exclusive Prospecting Order** is a regulatory instrument issued by the **Ministry of Mines and Mining Development** under the provisions of the **[[Mines and Minerals Act]]**, granting a designated applicant the sole right to explore a specified mineral resource area for a defined period; it delineates the scope of permissible activities, obliges the holder to adhere to environmental and safety standards, and requires the submission of detailed work programmes and financial assurances, thereby ensuring that the exploration is conducted responsibly while protecting the state’s mineral assets and promoting sustainable development.
