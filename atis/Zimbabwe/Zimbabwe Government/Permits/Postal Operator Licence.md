## Postal Operator Licence

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[Postal and Telecommunications Act]] 
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 60 to 120 working days


## Description
The **Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ)** issues a Postal Operator Licence under the **[[Postal and Telecommunications Act]] (Chapter 12:05)**, which authorises entities to provide a full range of postal services—including the collection, sorting, transport, and delivery of letters, parcels, and other mail items—within Zimbabwe and across international borders; the licence also mandates compliance with standards for service quality, security, and consumer protection, requires the holder to maintain appropriate financial and technical capacity, and obliges regular reporting to **POTRAZ** to ensure that the postal network operates efficiently, reliably, and in alignment with national regulatory objectives.
