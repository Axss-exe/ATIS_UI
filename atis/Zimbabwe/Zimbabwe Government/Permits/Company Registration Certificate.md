## Company Registration Certificate

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Registrar of Companies and Other Business Entities]]
**Legislation**: Companies and Other Business Entities Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: None
**Processing Time**: 5 to 7 working days


## Description
The **Company Registration Certificate** is the formal, government‑issued proof that a business has been duly incorporated and is legally recognised under the **Companies and Other Business Entities Act**, confirming that the entity has satisfied all statutory requirements and is entered on the public register; it is issued by the **Registrar of Companies and Other Business Entities**, and it details the company’s registered name, unique registration number, date of incorporation, registered office address, and the nature of its business activities, thereby serving as the essential credential for opening bank accounts, entering contracts, and complying with regulatory obligations.
