## Mineral Marketing Registration

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[MMCZ Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 10 to 14 working days


## Description
The **Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)** issues a Mineral Marketing Registration under the **[[MMCZ Act]]** as a compulsory licence that authorises mining entities to legally market, sell, and export their mineral products through the corporation’s regulated channels; the registration confirms that the holder has complied with all statutory requirements, including proof of ownership, environmental compliance, and payment of applicable royalties, thereby ensuring transparent trade, safeguarding national revenue, and facilitating access to both domestic and international markets while maintaining strict oversight of the country’s mineral resources.
