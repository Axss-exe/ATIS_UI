## Water Works Approval

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe National Water Authority (ZINWA)]]
**Legislation**: Water Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 30 working days


## Description
The **Water Works Approval** is a regulatory permit granted by the **Zimbabwe National Water Authority (ZINWA)** under the provisions of the Water Act, authorising the design, construction, operation and maintenance of water supply schemes, irrigation systems and related infrastructure; it ensures that projects meet technical standards, environmental safeguards and sustainable water‑resource management criteria, and it obliges applicants to comply with monitoring, reporting and fee‑payment requirements throughout the life of the works.


## Standard Operating Procedure
  - "1. Prepare proposal"
  - "2. Complete application"
  - "3. Submit documents"
  - "4. Pay application fees"
  - "5. Technical review/Due diligence"
  - "6. Site inspection (if applicable)"
  - "7. Board/Committee approval"
  - "8. Receive licence"

  - "Certificate of Incorporation"

  - "Business Plan/Proposal"
  - "Director IDs"
  - "Tax Clearance"
  - "Proof of Application Fee Payment"
  - "Hydrological Report"
  - "Proof of Land Ownership"
  - "Pump Specifications"
