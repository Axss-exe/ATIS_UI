## Standards Certification (where applicable)

**Processing Time**: 30 to 60 working days


## Description
The **Standards Association of Zimbabwe (SAZ)** administers a comprehensive **Standards Certification** scheme within its national Standards Framework, granting certification to products, processes and management systems that demonstrably comply with Zimbabwean and, where relevant, international standards; this certification serves as a mark of quality and safety, facilitating market access, consumer confidence and regulatory compliance, while also supporting local manufacturers in achieving competitive parity and fostering continuous improvement through periodic audits and technical assessments.
