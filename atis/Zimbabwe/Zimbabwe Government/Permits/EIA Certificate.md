## EIA Certificate

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Environmental Management Agency (EMA)]]
**Legislation**: [[Environmental Management Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 60 working days


## Description
The **EIA Certificate** is an official approval issued by the **Environmental Management Agency (EMA)** pursuant to the **[[Environmental Management Act]]**, confirming that a proposed development has undergone a comprehensive Environmental Impact Assessment and meets all statutory requirements for environmental protection; the certificate details the specific mitigation measures, monitoring obligations, and compliance timelines that the project must adhere to, thereby ensuring that potential adverse effects on ecosystems, public health, and natural resources are minimized and managed throughout the lifecycle of the activity.


## Standard Operating Procedure
  - "1. Submit Prospectus"
  - "2. EMA approves terms of reference"
  - "3. Conduct EIA study"
  - "4. Submit EIA Report"
  - "5. Public consultation review"
  - "6. EMA site verification"
  - "7. Issue certificate"

## Required Documents
  - "EIA Prospectus"
  - "Full EIA Report"
  - "Proof of Public Consultation"
  - "Consultant Certification"
  - "Project Maps"

## Common Bottlenecks
  - "Inadequate public consultation evidence"
  - "Rejection of Prospectus"
  - "EMA capacity limits for site visits"
