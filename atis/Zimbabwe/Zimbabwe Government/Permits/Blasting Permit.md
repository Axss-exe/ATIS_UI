## Blasting Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe Republic Police]]
**Legislation**: Explosives Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: Explosives Storage Permit
**Processing Time**: 14 to 21 working days


## Description
The **Blasting Permit** is an official authorization issued by the **Zimbabwe Republic Police** under the provisions of the **Explosives Act**, granting qualified individuals or companies the legal right to conduct controlled detonations for mining, construction, or quarrying activities; the permit outlines strict safety protocols, specifies the geographic area and time frame for the blast, mandates the presence of certified explosives handlers, and requires detailed notification to local authorities and emergency services to ensure public safety and compliance with national regulations.


## Standard Operating Procedure
  - "1. Application to local ZRP"
  - "2. ZRP forwards to CID Minerals/Explosives unit"
  - "3. Magazine inspection"
  - "4. Security vetting of blaster"
  - "5. Issuance of permit"

  - "Tax Clearance"
  - "Proof of Application Fee Payment"
  - "Approved Magazine Plans"
  - "Blaster's License"
  - "Police Clearance"
  - "Security Guard Contracts"
