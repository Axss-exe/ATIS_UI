## Hazardous Substance Licence

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Environmental Management Agency (EMA)]]
**Legislation**: [[Environmental Management Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 60 working days


## Description
The **Hazardous Substance Licence** is a regulatory permit granted by the **Environmental Management Agency (EMA)** under the **[[Environmental Management Act]]** that authorises businesses and individuals to store, handle, transport, or dispose of hazardous substances in compliance with strict safety and environmental standards; applicants must demonstrate robust risk‑management plans, secure containment facilities, and ongoing monitoring procedures, while the licence imposes conditions such as regular reporting, emergency response protocols, and periodic inspections to ensure that the handling of dangerous chemicals does not jeopardise public health or ecological integrity.

  - "Project Maps"
