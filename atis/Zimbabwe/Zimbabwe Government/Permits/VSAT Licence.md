## VSAT Licence

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ)]]
**Legislation**: [[Postal and Telecommunications Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Company Registration
**Processing Time**: 60 to 120 working days


## Description
The **VSAT Licence** is a regulatory authorization granted by the **Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ)** pursuant to the **Postal and Telecommunications Act [Chapter 12:05]**, which permits the deployment and operation of Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) satellite communication systems within Zimbabwe’s jurisdiction; the licence outlines the technical standards, spectrum allocations, and service quality requirements that operators must adhere to, mandates compliance with national security and data protection protocols, and requires periodic reporting and fee payments to ensure that satellite services are delivered reliably, competitively, and in alignment with the country’s broader telecommunications policy objectives.
