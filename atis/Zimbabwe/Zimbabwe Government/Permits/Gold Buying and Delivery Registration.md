## Gold Buying / Delivery Registration

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Fidelity Gold Refinery]]
**Legislation**: [[Gold Trade Regulations]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Mining Rights
**Processing Time**: 14 working days


## Description
The **Gold Buying / Delivery Registration** issued by **Fidelity Gold Refinery** under the **[[Gold Trade Regulations]]** serves as a formal record that authorizes the refinery to purchase, receive, and dispatch gold on behalf of licensed traders, ensuring that each transaction complies with statutory purity, weight, and provenance standards; the registration requires detailed documentation of the buyer’s credentials, the source and assay of the metal, and a traceable delivery schedule, thereby providing regulatory bodies with transparent oversight while safeguarding market integrity and preventing illicit trade.
