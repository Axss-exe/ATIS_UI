## Environmental Impact Assessment

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Environmental Management Agency (EMA)]]
**Legislation**: [[Environmental Management Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Prospecting Rights
**Processing Time**: 60 working days


## Description
The **Environmental Management Agency (EMA)** issues an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) in accordance with the **[[Environmental Management Act]]**, a comprehensive regulatory framework that mandates a systematic evaluation of potential environmental consequences before any major project proceeds. The EIA process, overseen by **EMA**, requires developers to identify, predict, and mitigate adverse effects on ecosystems, water quality, air emissions, and community health, while also considering cumulative impacts and alternative project designs. By integrating scientific analysis, stakeholder consultation, and transparent reporting, the assessment ensures that decision‑makers have a robust evidence base to balance economic development with the preservation of natural resources and the well‑being of current and future generations.
