## Wildlife Permit

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[ZimParks]]
**Legislation**: Parks and Wildlife Act
**Prerequisite Permits**: EIA Certificate
**Processing Time**: 14 to 30 working days


## Description
The **Wildlife Permit** issued by **ZimParks** under the **Parks and Wildlife Act** authorises individuals or organisations to legally capture, transport, retain, or otherwise manage designated wildlife species for purposes such as research, conservation, education, or controlled commercial activities, provided that all stipulated conditions—including species‑specific quotas, seasonal restrictions, and habitat protection measures—are strictly adhered to; the permit also mandates comprehensive record‑keeping, regular reporting to **ZimParks**, and compliance with any additional safeguards designed to ensure the sustainable use and long‑term preservation of Zimbabwe’s natural heritage.
