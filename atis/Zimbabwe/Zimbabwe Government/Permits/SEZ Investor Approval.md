## SEZ Investor Approval

**Country**: Zimbabwe
**Issuing Entity**: [[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency (ZIDA)]]
**Legislation**: [[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency Act]]
**Prerequisite Permits**: Investment License
**Processing Time**: 7 to 21 working days


## Description
The **SEZ Investor Approval** is a formal authorization granted by the **Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency (ZIDA)** under the provisions of the **[[Zimbabwe Investment and Development Agency Act]]**, confirming that a proposed investment meets all regulatory, fiscal and strategic criteria for operation within a designated Special Economic Zone; the approval process involves a comprehensive review of the project’s economic impact, compliance with zoning regulations, and alignment with national development objectives, after which the agency issues a certificate that enables the investor to access preferential tax regimes, streamlined customs procedures and other incentives designed to foster export‑oriented growth and job creation in Zimbabwe’s priority sectors.
