# Energy Regulatory Authority Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Zimbabwe Energy Regulatory Authority (ZERA)]]"


## Description
Established ZERA to regulate the electricity and petroleum sectors. It handles IPP licensing frameworks and infrastructure tariff approval mechanisms, which are critical for investors building off-grid or captive solar operations.
