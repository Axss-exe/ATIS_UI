# Revenue Authority Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA)]]"


## Description
Establishes ZIMRA as the principal agency responsible for collecting national revenue and enforcing tax/customs laws. It provides the statutory backend for corporate tax obligations, audits, and border custom clearances.
