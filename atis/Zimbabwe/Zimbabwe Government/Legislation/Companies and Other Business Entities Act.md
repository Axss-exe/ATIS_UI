# Companies and Other Business Entities Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Registrar of Companies]]"


## Description
Modernized corporate governance in Zimbabwe (replacing the old Companies Act). Introduces provisions for electronic registration, mandatory beneficial ownership tracking, and stricter protection for minority shareholders.
