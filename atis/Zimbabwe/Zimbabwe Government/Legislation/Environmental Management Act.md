# Environmental Management Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Environmental Management Agency (EMA)]]"


## Description
Governs the sustainable management of natural resources and the protection of the environment. For investors, particularly in mining and infrastructure, its most critical provision is the mandatory requirement to obtain an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) certificate prior to project commencement. It also defines waste management standards and pollution control limits.
