# MMCZ Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)]]"


## Description
Establishes the MMCZ and grants it an exclusive mandate to act as the sole marketing and selling agent for all minerals produced in Zimbabwe (excluding gold and silver). This mandates that all covered mineral export contracts must go through the MMCZ, adding statutory commission layers to the export pipeline.
