# Precious Stones Trade Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Ministry of Mines and Mining Development]], [[Zimbabwe Republic Police (ZRP)]]"


## Description
Restricts and regulates the commercial possession, sorting, and export tracking of precious stones (primarily diamonds and emeralds) under severe criminal penalty guidelines.
