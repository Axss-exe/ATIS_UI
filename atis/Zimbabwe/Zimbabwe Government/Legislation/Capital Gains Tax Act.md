# Capital Gains Tax Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA)]]"


## Description
Governs tax liability calculations on the disposal of specified capital assets (such as real estate or marketable securities), dictating corporate M&A restructuring costs.
