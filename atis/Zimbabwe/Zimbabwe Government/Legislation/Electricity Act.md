# Electricity Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Zimbabwe Energy Regulatory Authority (ZERA)]]"
  - "[[Zimbabwe Electricity Supply Authority (ZESA)]]"


## Description
Governs the generation, transmission, and supply of electricity. It acts as the legal backbone for Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) between private Independent Power Producers (IPPs) and the state grid.
