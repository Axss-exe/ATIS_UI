# Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe Act

**Status**: Active
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe (RBZ)]]"


## Description
Dictates the monetary policy framework, banking regulations, and exchange controls. Crucial for FDI tracking of forex retention ratios, dividend repatriation, and cross-border capital registrations via Exchange Control directives.
