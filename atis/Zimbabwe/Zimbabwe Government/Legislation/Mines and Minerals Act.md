# Mines and Minerals Act

**Status**: Active - Under Revision
**Enforced By**: 
  - "[[Ministry of Mines and Mining Development]]"


## Description
The foundational law for extractive operations. Vests all mineral rights in the President and sets out the processes for acquiring prospecting licenses, Exclusive Prospecting Orders (EPOs), and mining leases.
