## Manicaland

## Summary
Eastern border province with strong agricultural, forestry, mining, and tourism sectors. Major resources include gold, diamonds, timber, and fertile agricultural land. Strategic importance stems from cross-border trade with Mozambique, hydropower potential, and diversified natural resources.
