## Masvingo

## Summary
Historically significant province characterized by agriculture, mining, tourism, and water infrastructure. Home to Great Zimbabwe, Tokwe-Mukosi Dam, and significant gold and lithium prospects. Strategic opportunities exist in irrigation, mining, renewable energy, and agro-processing.
