## Mashonaland Central

## Summary
Major mining and agricultural province with significant gold deposits and emerging lithium potential. Key activities include mining, tobacco farming, and commercial agriculture. Strategic value is driven by mineral wealth, agricultural productivity, and proximity to national economic centers.
