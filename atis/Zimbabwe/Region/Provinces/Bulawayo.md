## Bulawayo

## Summary
Zimbabwe's second-largest city and industrial hub. Historically the country's manufacturing center with strong transport, logistics, engineering, and commercial infrastructure. Strategic importance derives from industrial capacity, rail connectivity, and regional trade access.
