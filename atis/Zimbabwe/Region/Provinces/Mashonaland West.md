## Mashonaland West

## Summary
Strategic agricultural and mining province bordering Zambia. Key economic activities include tobacco production, gold mining, lithium exploration, and commercial agriculture. Home to Kariba-related energy infrastructure, major transport corridors, and growing investment opportunities in mining, energy, and agro-processing.
