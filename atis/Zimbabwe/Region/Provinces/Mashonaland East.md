## Mashonaland East

## Summary
Economically diverse province with agriculture, mining, manufacturing, and tourism activities. Benefits from proximity to Harare and hosts significant gold mining and commercial farming operations. Strategic opportunities include industrial expansion, logistics, and value-added agriculture.
