## Harare

## Summary
Zimbabwe's capital province and primary political, financial, commercial, and administrative center. Hosts national government institutions, regulatory authorities, major corporations, financial services, and diplomatic missions. Strategic importance is national-level, serving as the country's principal decision-making and investment hub.
