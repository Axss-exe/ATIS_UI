## Matabeleland South

## Summary
Border province with strong livestock, mining, and cross-border trade activities. Contains significant gold and lithium prospects and serves as a gateway to Botswana and South Africa. Strategic importance stems from trade corridors, mineral resources, and regional connectivity.
