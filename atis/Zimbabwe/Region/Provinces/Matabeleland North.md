## Matabeleland North

## Summary
Resource-rich province known for coal, gold, tourism, wildlife conservation, and energy assets. Hosts Victoria Falls, Hwange National Park, and Hwange Power Station. Strategic importance derives from tourism, power generation, mining, and regional trade connectivity.
