## Midlands

## Summary
Centrally located province containing major mining, manufacturing, and energy assets. Hosts significant gold, chrome, iron, and platinum operations as well as industrial facilities in Gweru and Kwekwe. Strategic importance is enhanced by central geographic location and diversified economic activity.
