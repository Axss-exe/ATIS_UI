**Province**: [[Matabeleland North]]

## Summary
Tankahukwe is a modest rural settlement in northern Zimbabwe, positioned on the gently rolling savanna of the Mashonaland West province. The village lies a few kilometres off the main A1 highway that links the regional hub of Kadoma with the provincial capital of Chinhoyi, giving it limited but functional road access. The surrounding landscape is dominated by mixed grassland and scattered miombo woodland, supporting small‑scale subsistence farming of maize, sorghum and groundnuts,
