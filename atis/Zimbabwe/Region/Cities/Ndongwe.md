**Country**: [[Zimbabwe]]


## Summary
Ndongwe is a modest settlement in Mashonaland East, situated roughly 30 km east of Harare along the paved A2 highway to Mutare, giving it good road connectivity and easy access to the capital’s markets. The surrounding landscape is a gently rolling savanna with fertile, well‑drained soils that support mixed farming—principally maize, tobacco, and horticultural crops
