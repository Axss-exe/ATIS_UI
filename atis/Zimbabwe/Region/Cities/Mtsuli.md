## Summary
Mtsuli is a small rural settlement in Zimbabwe’s Midlands Province, situated on the banks of the seasonal Mtsuli River and roughly 30 km west of the larger town of Gokwe. The landscape consists of gently rolling savanna hills dotted with miombo woodland, interspersed with cultivated fields and grazing
