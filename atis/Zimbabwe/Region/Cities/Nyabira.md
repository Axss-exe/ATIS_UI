## Summary
Nyabira is a small town in Mashonaland West, situated about 30 km north of Harare along the A1 highway that links the capital with Chinhoyi and Bulawayo. The surrounding landscape is a mix of open savanna, gently rolling hills and irrigated farmland, dominated by tobacco, maize and horticultural plots that benefit from water supplied by the Nyabira Dam. The settlement functions as a service hub for these farms, offering a market, petrol station, primary school, health centre and a police post, while a nearby railway siding provides freight access.
