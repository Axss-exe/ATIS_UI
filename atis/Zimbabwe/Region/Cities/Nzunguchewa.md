## Summary
Nzunguchewa is a small rural settlement in Mashonaland West, roughly 30 km north of Chinhoyi, set amid gently rolling savanna and black cotton soils that support smallholder maize, tobacco and groundnut farms. The village is accessed via a paved secondary road that connects to the A1 highway, and it benefits from basic infrastructure including electricity, a primary school and a weekly market where local produce and craft goods are traded.
