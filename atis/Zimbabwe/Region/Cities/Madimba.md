## Summary
Madimba is a modest town in Kongo Central province of the Democratic Republic of Congo, situated on the main Kinshasa‑Matadi corridor about 120 km southwest of the capital. The surrounding landscape is a mix of low‑lying tropical savanna, gently rolling hills and patches of forest, with the Congo River basin influencing the climate
