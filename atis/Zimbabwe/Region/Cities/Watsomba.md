## Summary
Watsomba is a modest growth‑point town in Manicaland’s Chipinge district, set amid the rolling, high‑rainfall hills of the Eastern Highlands and a short distance from the Bvumba mountain range. The settlement is linked by a paved road to Chipinge and the A2 highway, providing reasonable access to Mutare and the Mozambique border.
