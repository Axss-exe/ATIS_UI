**City**: Machacha
**Province**: [[Manicaland]]
**Country**: [[Zimbabwe]]


## Summary
Machacha is a modest settlement in Mashonaland West, roughly 30 km west of Harare along the A1 highway that links the capital with Bulawayo. The terrain is gently rolling savanna punctuated by miombo woodland and small tributaries that feed the Mazowe River, supporting irrigated smallholder farms that grow maize, groundnuts and tobacco.
