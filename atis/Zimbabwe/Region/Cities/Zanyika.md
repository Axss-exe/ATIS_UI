## Summary
Zanyika is a modest settlement in north‑western Tanzania, situated within the Shinyanga Region’s Kahama District on the edge of the Central Plateau. The town lies along the paved Shinyanga‑Mwanza highway, providing reliable road links to regional hubs and the nearby mining centre of Kahama, while a secondary gravel road connects it to surrounding villages. The landscape is a mix of gently rolling savanna and cultivated fields, with fertile soils supporting maize, beans and sorghum, and small‑scale livestock grazing.
