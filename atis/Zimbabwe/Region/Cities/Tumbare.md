## Summary
Tumbare is a modest rural settlement situated roughly 30 km west of Harare in Mashonaland West, Zimbabwe, where the A1 highway and a secondary paved road provide direct links to the capital and surrounding towns such as Norton and Samanga. The landscape consists of gently rolling savanna
