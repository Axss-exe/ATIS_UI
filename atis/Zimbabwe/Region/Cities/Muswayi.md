## Summary
Muswayi is a modest settlement in Mashonaland West, situated roughly 30 km north of Kadoma along the secondary road that links the town to the Great Dyke mineral corridor. The surrounding landscape is a mix of open savanna and gently rolling farmland, interspersed with low‑lying granite outcrops that host small-scale gold and nickel mining operations.
