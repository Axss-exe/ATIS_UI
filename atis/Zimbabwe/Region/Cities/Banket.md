## Summary
Banket is a small town in Mashonaland West, situated about 100 km west of Harare and 30 km north‑east of Kadoma, on the Harare‑Bulawayo railway and the A1 highway. The surrounding landscape is a gently rolling savanna punctuated by granite outcrops of the Great Dyke, which hosts gold and nickel deposits that drive the local mining sector. Adjacent farms produce cotton, maize, tobacco and horticultural crops, benefitting from irrigation supplied by the Mazowe River and nearby dams.
