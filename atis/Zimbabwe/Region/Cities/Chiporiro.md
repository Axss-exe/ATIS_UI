## Summary
Chipariro is a modest rural settlement in Mashonaland West, situated about 200 km north‑west of Harare and a few kilometres off the A1 highway that links Harare to Karoi and Chinhoyi. The surrounding landscape is characterised by gently undulating savanna, open fields and patches of miombo woodland, with a seasonal stream that supplies water for irrigation and livestock. Smallholder farms dominate
