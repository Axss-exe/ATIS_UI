## Summary
Ndakaripei is a modest rural settlement in Mashonaland West, situated roughly 150 km north of Harare along the A1 highway between Karoi and Chinhoyi. The surrounding landscape is a mix of gently rolling savanna and fertile valleys cut by the Mupfure River, supporting small‑holder maize, tobacco and groundnut farms as well as cattle grazing. The village is linked to the national electricity
