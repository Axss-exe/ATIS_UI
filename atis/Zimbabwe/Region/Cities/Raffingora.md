## Summary
Raffingora is a modest town in Mashonaland West, situated on the A1 highway roughly 80 km north of Harare and 30 km south of Chinhoyi, giving it good road connectivity and a railway stop on the Harare–Bulawayo line. The surrounding landscape is a gently rolling, fertile plateau
