**Province**: [[Mashonaland West]]
**Country**: [[Zimbabwe]]


## Summary
Chitehere is a modest rural settlement situated on the undulating plateau of Mashonaland West, roughly 30 km east of the larger town of Kadoma and linked by a paved secondary road to the A1 highway. The landscape is characterised by open savanna grasslands interspersed with scattered acacia and iron‑rich outcrops of the Great Dyke, providing limited but exploitable mineral deposits, chiefly gold and chrome. Agriculture dominates the local economy, with smallholder farms cultivating maize, sorghum and groundnuts, while cattle grazing is common on the surrounding communal lands
