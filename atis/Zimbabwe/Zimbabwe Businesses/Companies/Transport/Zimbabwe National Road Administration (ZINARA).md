# Zimbabwe National Road Administration (ZINARA)

## Summary
ZINARA’s mission is to develop, maintain, and manage Zimbabwe’s road network through sustainable financing and efficient toll collection, thereby supporting economic growth and social development.  The agency’s operations are centred on a toll‑road system that generates revenue for road upkeep and new projects, with toll plazas and electronic collection points across major highways. ZINARA administers the allocation of these funds to routine maintenance, rehabilitation, and construction of new road sections, coordinating with provincial road authorities and private contractors. It oversees the implementation of road‑safety initiatives, monitors traffic volumes, and conducts periodic audits to ensure compliance with national standards. To enhance service delivery, ZINARA offers a mobile application that provides e‑recruitment, tolling queries, route management, pothole tracking, and road‑condition surveys, enabling users to report hazards and access real‑time information. The app’s features support data‑driven decision‑making and facilitate public participation in infrastructure monitoring. Additionally, the organization engages in stakeholder outreach, public‑private partnerships, and capacity‑building programmes to improve the overall efficiency of the road‑transport sector.
