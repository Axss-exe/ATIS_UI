# National Railways of Zimbabwe (NRZ)

## Summary
We promote the transformation of the regional economy through provision of innovative, seamless, reliable and cost‑effective logistics solutions for goods and passengers.  National Railways of Zimbabwe (NRZ) operates a mixed‑modal rail network that includes general freight services for the mining, energy, industrial and manufacturing sectors, as well as a dedicated parcel brand (Railstar) that moves parcels within Zimbabwe and to neighboring ports in Mozambique and Botswana on an express overnight schedule.  An intercity passenger service is designed to link major cities and towns, although all passenger trains are currently suspended.  NRZ’s infrastructure comprises a marshaling yard in Harare and a network of lines that support both freight and passenger movements, with plans for future investment and modernization overseen by the Chief Planning Manager in Bulawayo.  The organization also engages in tender processes, offers quotation services for logistics, and maintains a focus on health and safety, environmental sustainability, and customer‑oriented service delivery as part of its broader operational framework.
