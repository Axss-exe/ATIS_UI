# Fidelity Gold Refinery (FGR)

## Summary
Fidelity Gold Refinery (FGR) aims to deliver high‑purity gold products while upholding rigorous standards of ethical sourcing, environmental stewardship, and industry compliance. FGR operates a vertically integrated network that sources raw gold from licensed mines, scrap dealers, and certified recyclers across multiple jurisdictions. The company’s primary facilities—located in North America, Europe, and Asia—employ conventional pyrometallurgical and hydrometallurgical processes to melt, assay, and refine gold to 99.99 % purity or higher. Certified analysts conduct continuous quality control, and the refined metal is cast into bars, coins, and specialty alloys for sale to bullion dealers, jewelry manufacturers, and institutional investors. FGR maintains a compliance framework aligned with the London Bullion Market Association (LBMA) Good Delivery standards, the Conflict‑Free Sourcing Initiative, and relevant anti‑money‑laundering regulations. The refinery also offers secure storage, logistics, and forward‑selling services, leveraging a digital platform for transaction tracking and client reporting. Environmental measures include waste‑heat recovery, water‑recycling systems, and periodic audits to reduce the carbon footprint of its operations.
