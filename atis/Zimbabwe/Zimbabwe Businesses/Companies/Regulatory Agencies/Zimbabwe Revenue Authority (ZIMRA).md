# Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA)

## Summary
The Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) is mandated to administer, collect and enforce domestic taxes and customs duties, promote compliance, and support national revenue mobilization in line with government fiscal policy. ZIMRA operates a suite of digital platforms, including the e‑Tip portal for electronic tax invoicing, the ASYCUDA World system for customs clearance, the TaRMS self‑service portal for tax registration, and the e‑Tariff portal for tariff information. It provides online services such as e‑CDRMS for electronic customs documentation, e‑CDRMS for customs bond management, and a whistle‑blower portal for reporting non‑compliance. The authority publishes regular updates on exchange rates, tax tables, and downloadable forms (e.g., annual reports, bond applications). Recent activities highlighted on the ZIMRA website include engagement with University of Zimbabwe finance students on tax administration, stakeholder breakfast meetings, infrastructure upgrades at border posts, and enforcement actions such as the interception of smuggled meat. ZIMRA also runs public awareness campaigns on VAT obligations, customs dog operations, and International Customs Day events. Contact directories, downloadable resources, and a board ethics charter are available to support transparency and stakeholder interaction.
