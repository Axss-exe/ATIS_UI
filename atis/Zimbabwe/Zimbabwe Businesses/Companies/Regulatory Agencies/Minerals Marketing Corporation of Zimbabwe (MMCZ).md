# Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ)

## Summary
The mission of the Minerals Marketing Corporation of Zimbabwe (MMCZ) is to maximise returns to all stakeholders through effective surveillance and efficient marketing of the country’s mineral resources. MMCZ operates as the sole marketing and selling agent for every Zimbabwe‑produced mineral except gold and silver, handling contract negotiation, Kimberley Process certification, logistics, and product distribution for commodities such as nickel, ferrochrome, steel, and platinum‑group metals. It maintains a daily‑updated mineral‑price platform to provide traders and investors with current market data, and it runs an inspectorate function that monitors export flows, enforces compliance, and curtails illicit trade, as highlighted by recent lithium‑truck interceptions. The corporation also evaluates rough and polished diamonds, markets directly to regional and international buyers (including Belgium, India, Dubai, and South Africa), and promotes value‑addition initiatives such as local beneficiation of lithium and other critical minerals. MMCZ publishes quarterly performance figures, reporting export volumes, revenue growth, and sector‑specific trends, and it engages with producers, buyers, and the broader public through news releases, social‑media updates, and stakeholder outreach programs.
