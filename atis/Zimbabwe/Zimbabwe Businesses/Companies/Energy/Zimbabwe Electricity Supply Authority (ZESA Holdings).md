# Zimbabwe Electricity Supply Authority (ZESA Holdings)

## Summary
The mission of Zimbabwe Electricity Supply Authority (ZESA Holdings) is to ensure the reliable, safe and affordable supply of electricity to support socio‑economic development and improve the quality of life for all Zimbabweans.  ZESA Holdings oversees the generation, transmission and distribution of electricity across the country through its principal subsidiaries—ZESA Power, which operates the nation’s hydro‑electric and thermal power stations; ZESA Transmission, responsible for the high‑voltage grid that interconnects generation sites with regional distributors; and ZESA Distribution, which manages the low‑voltage networks that deliver power to residential, commercial and industrial customers. The company also coordinates with independent power producers and regional power pools to augment capacity and enhance grid stability. Its operations include routine maintenance of infrastructure, load‑forecasting, tariff setting in line with regulatory guidelines, and implementation of investment programmes aimed at modernising ageing assets and expanding access in underserved areas. ZESA Holdings works under the oversight of the Ministry of Energy and Power Development and complies with national electricity regulations, while pursuing reforms intended to improve financial sustainability, operational efficiency and service reliability.
