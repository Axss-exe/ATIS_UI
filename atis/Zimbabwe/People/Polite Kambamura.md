# Polite Kambamura

## Bio
A mining engineer and politician serving as Minister of Mines and Mining Development since December 2025. Previously served as Deputy Minister since 2018. Member of Parliament for Sanyati Constituency (ZANU-PF). Has experience working in mines in Zimbabwe and South Africa.

## Current Position(s)
Minister of Mines and Mining Development, Member of Parliament for Sanyati

## Education
- BSc Honours in Mining Engineering (University of Zimbabwe), MBA, PhD in Mining Engineering, Master's in Natural Resource Management, Master's in Public Policy
