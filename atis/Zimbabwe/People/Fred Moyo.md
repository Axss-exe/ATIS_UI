# Fred Moyo

## Bio
Hon. Eng. Fred Moyo is an accomplished mining engineer, seasoned policymaker, and legislator serving as Deputy Minister of Mines and Mining Development. He rose through the ranks to managing director in various mines in Zimbabwe and has extensive experience in gold, asbestos, coal, and chrome mining.
