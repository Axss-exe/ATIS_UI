# Mthuli Ncube

## Bio
Prof. Mthuli Ncube is the Minister of Finance and Economic Development. He has a background in academia, banking, investments, and public policy. Former Vice President and Chief Economist of the African Development Bank Group. Served as a Professor at Oxford University and Dean at Wits Business School.
