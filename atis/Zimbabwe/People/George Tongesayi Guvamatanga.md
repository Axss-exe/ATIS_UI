# George Tongesayi Guvamatanga

## Bio
George Guvamatanga is a technocrat and banker serving as Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Finance since 2018. Worked for Barclays Bank Zimbabwe for 30 years, ending as Managing Director. Involved in designing the Mutapa Investment Fund.

## Current Position(s)
Permanent Secretary, Ministry of Finance and Investment Promotion
