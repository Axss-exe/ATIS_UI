# [[Subspace Energy Hub]]

## Summary
The mission of Subspace Energy Hub is to accelerate the development, prototyping, and deployment of integrated above‑ground and underground sustainable energy technologies that enable greener energy use, storage, and delivery for future societies. The Hub operates from the VersuchsStollen Hagerbach Test Gallery in Switzerland, providing a model ecosystem where partners can test and validate solutions such as DC microgrids, battery‑electric vehicle (BEV) systems for tunnelling and mining, hydrogen storage, and net‑zero construction site concepts. Its workstreams cover safety and risk management, electrification of heavy‑duty construction equipment, and energy‑storage strategies that combine solar, wind, and fuel‑cell generation with battery systems. Subspace Energy Hub facilitates knowledge exchange through podcasts recorded at industry sites, expert roundtables, and regular events that showcase project updates and partner initiatives. The organization maintains an active partnership network, recently adding AngloGold Ashanti and hosting delegations from Chile, while also coordinating virtual meetings to discuss electrification progress in mines. Communication channels include a website, news releases, and contact points for the VersuchsStollen Hagerbach AG facility, ensuring transparent access to its activities and collaborative opportunities.
