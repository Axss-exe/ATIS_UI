# [[RID Batterie]]

## Summary
RID Batterie’s mission is to deliver reliable, high‑performance and sustainable battery solutions that meet the diverse energy needs of industrial, commercial and export markets. The company operates as a family‑owned manufacturer and distributor of a wide portfolio of batteries for standby power, telecommunications, energy storage, and specialized equipment such as generators and UPS systems. It offers custom‑engineered battery packs, from initial concept through planning, production and delivery, supported by a dedicated battery laboratory that inspects, refurbishes and recycles used units. RID Batterie maintains a large inventory that enables rapid fulfillment across the DACH region (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) as well as broader European and export markets. Physical sales points are located in Leimen and Kirchardt, and the firm collaborates with local partners and retailers to extend its reach. The business also provides spare‑part sales, technical consulting, and after‑sales support, emphasizing short lead times, clear solutions and a partnership‑oriented approach. Participation in industry exhibitions such as GITEX AI Central Asia, Bauma and E.I.S. Profitreff showcases its product range and reinforces its market presence. The organization employs over 1,000 satisfied customers, 340 distinct battery types, and leverages more than 15 years of experience in battery technology and recycling compliance.
