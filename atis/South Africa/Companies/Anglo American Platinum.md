# Anglo American Platinum

## Summary
Anglo American’s mission is to “re‑imagine mining to improve people’s lives.” The company operates a diversified global portfolio of mining assets that produce essential metals and minerals—including copper, premium iron ore, nickel, diamonds, steelmaking coal and crop nutrients such as polyhalite—supporting sectors ranging from smartphones and electric vehicles to infrastructure and renewable‑energy technologies. Its operations are organized around a Sustainable Mining Plan and a “FutureSmart Mining™” framework that integrates technology, digitisation and sustainability across the value chain. Recent strategic moves include the agreed sale of its Australian steelmaking‑coal portfolio to Dhilmar Limited for up to US $3.875 billion, and a merger with Teck Resources to form “Anglo Teck,” a critical‑minerals leader targeting copper growth and US $2.2 billion of synergies. Anglo American publishes quarterly production reports, annual integrated reports (2025) and full‑year results, and maintains a corporate focus on trusted leadership, environmental health and thriving communities. The firm also emphasizes inclusion, diversity and career development through its “FutureSmart Mining” and corporate communications platforms.
