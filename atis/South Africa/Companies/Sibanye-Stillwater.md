# Sibanye-Stillwater

## Summary
Sibanye‑Stillwater’s mission is to responsibly deliver sustainable value for shareholders, employees, and the communities in which it operates by extracting and processing precious metals with an emphasis on safety, environmental stewardship, and ethical practices. The company’s operations span two continents and focus on gold, platinum‑group metals (PGM) and copper. In South Africa, Sibanye‑Stillwater manages a portfolio of deep‑level gold mines—including Mponeng, TauTona, Driefontein, and Kusasalethu—as well as surface and underground PGM assets at Rustenburg, Marikana, and the Zondereinde complex. These sites are supported by on‑site processing plants that produce doré, refined gold, and PGM concentrates. In the United States, the firm operates the Stillwater Mine in Montana, a primary source of palladium and platinum, and the East Boulder Mine in Idaho, which supplies copper concentrate for downstream smelting. The company also runs a recycling business
