# Impala Platinum Holdings Limited

## Summary
Our purpose is to create a better future by delivering responsible metals production, supporting inclusive economic opportunities, and protecting the natural environment. Impala Platinum Holdings Limited (Implats) is a fully integrated producer of platinum‑group metals (PGMs) with six mining operations, a refining and processing network, and a dedicated refining business (Impala Refining Services). The core mining assets are located on the Bushveld Complex in South Africa and the Great Dyke in Zimbabwe—both of the world’s largest PGM‑bearing ore bodies—and a supplemental operation on the Canadian Shield. The Group employs more than 66 000 people across these sites, with additional staff supporting corporate, sustainability, and community functions. Implats’ products are exported to key markets in Japan, China, the United States and Europe, where they are used in industrial, medical and electronic applications, including fuel‑cell technologies for decarbonisation. The company reports its financial and ESG performance through regular interim and annual results, maintains a B‑BEE compliance framework, and engages stakeholders through investor communications, media releases, and community‑focused programmes such as employee accommodation and local sports initiatives.
