# DBE Services

## Summary
DBE Services’ mission is to serve, support and inspire schools, operating from a Christian foundation that seeks to embody the principles of grace, hope, love and service. The organisation is wholly owned by six Diocesan Boards of Education and Finance (Blackburn, Carlisle, Chester, Liverpool, Manchester and York), whose directors also sit on the company board, giving it a collective governance structure that represents nearly 850 Church of England schools and academies across the North of England.  DBE Services provides a portfolio of services that includes capital‑building support, routine maintenance and servicing, school‑improvement programmes, and leadership development for Christian school leaders.  It offers advice and guidance on an on‑call basis, and any surplus generated is reinvested into the diocesan boards and their schools.  The company also acts as a forum for joint working among dioceses, a consolidated voice on national education matters, and a platform for generating income while protecting and developing the Christian distinctiveness of church schools.  Contact details are publicly listed (phone 01254 958850, email [protected], address Clayton House, Walker Office Park, Blackburn).
