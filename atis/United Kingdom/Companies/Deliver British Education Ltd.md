# Deliver British Education Ltd

## Summary
Deliver British Education Ltd (DBE) states its mission as delivering British‑style education and comprehensive support services that enable students worldwide to achieve academic success, develop critical skills, and access global opportunities.  DBE operates a multi‑faceted platform that integrates the British curriculum with existing school programmes (Dual Curriculum), offers personalised tuition and homeschooling for expatriate and flexible learners, and runs structured international learning experiences such as the DBE Academic Summer Camp programmes in Riyadh and London. The company also organises cultural and educational school trips, British Model United Nations events, and preparation courses for international Olympiads. Additional services include career counselling, English‑skills development for non‑native speakers, and accredited professional‑training programmes for educators. DBE provides personalised consultations to identify student needs, creates bespoke learning plans, and delivers ongoing academic and personal‑development support.  Founded over nine years ago, DBE reports experience delivering tuition aligned with IGCSE and A‑Level standards, with a client base of thousands of students and parents. The organisation highlights outcomes such as strong exam performance, university admissions, and participation in extracurricular competitions, positioning itself as a provider of both curriculum delivery and experiential learning.
