# Huayou Cobalt

## Summary
Huayou Cobalt’s mission is to create value for customers, drive growth for employees, deliver returns for shareholders, and contribute to the broader industry while advancing a low‑carbon, environmentally‑friendly new‑energy ecosystem. The company operates a vertically integrated new‑energy supply chain that spans overseas resource development, domestic smelting, material manufacturing, and end‑of‑life recycling. Its core businesses include the extraction and processing of cobalt, nickel and lithium; the production of lithium‑ion battery precursors such as lithium sulfate and high‑nickel ternary cathodes; and the recycling of spent power batteries. Huayou maintains R&D centers covering 150,000 m², a workforce of over 4,500 employees, and more than 570 authorized patents. Internationally, it runs projects in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Zimbabwe, Indonesia (including the Pomalaa Industrial Park with new 40‑ton portal cranes), and Hungary, while its headquarters and major manufacturing sites are located in Tongxiang and Quzhou, China. Recent activities include a tripartite cooperation agreement with the Harare Institute of Technology, participation in the Ellen MacArthur Foundation’s circular‑economy white paper, and the launch of China’s first cross‑border finance‑lease benchmark for nickel‑smelting equipment.
