# Sinosteel Corporation

## Summary
Sinosteel Corporation’s mission is to sustainably develop, mine, process, and deliver metallurgical mineral resources while supporting China’s strategic industrial needs and global market demands. The company operates as a central state‑owned enterprise under the State‑Owned‑Asset Supervision and Administration Commission (SASAC) and ranks among China’s largest and most influential firms in the sector. Its core activities encompass the exploration, extraction, and beneficiation of metallurgical minerals, complemented by downstream processing to produce high‑grade raw materials for steelmaking and related industries. Sinosteel also maintains extensive trading operations, facilitating the import and export of mineral commodities, and provides research and development services aimed at improving resource efficiency and environmental performance. A worldwide sales network and integrated logistics system enable the firm to coordinate shipments, manage supply chains, and serve customers across multiple continents. In addition to its primary mining and processing functions, Sinosteel offers logistical support, including transportation, storage, and distribution services, thereby ensuring end‑to‑end coverage of the metallurgical resource value chain.
